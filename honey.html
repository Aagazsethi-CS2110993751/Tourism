Most Romantic And Best Honeymoon Destinations In India


HONEYMOON TRIP


Choosing the best destination from the top Honeymoon destinations in India is a task. Almost all of these places will look like best Honeymoon destinations in India that will make your little vacation after marriage, as memorable as possible. Here are the top honeymoon destinations in India.




Sonmarg, Jammu & Kashmir

Alleppey, Kerala

Dalhousie, Himachal Pradesh

Mussoorie, Uttarakhand

Kumarakom, Kerala

Shillong, Meghalaya

PLACES TO VISIT


here are the places you can explore


Sonmarg, Jammu & Kashmir


Among the many top honeymoon destinations in India, Sonmarg is among the top three. The valleys and the snow covered mountains of Sonmarg seem as if they have come straight out of a fairy tale.
Thajiwas Glacier, Zoji-La Pass, Whitewater rafting at Baltal, Kashmir Great Lakes Trek etc. are some places to explore




Alleppey, Kerala


Alleppey is among the most fascinating Honeymoon Places in South India and welcomes every newly married couple to spend some time in one of their private houseboats.
Alappuzha Beach, Backwaters, Krishnapuram Palace, Vembanad, Nehru Trophy Snake Boat race, Bay Island Driftwood Museum etc. are some places to explore here.




Dalhousie, Himachal Pradesh


Often referred to as ‘Mini Switzerland’, Dalhousie is the ideal place for honeymoon in the summers. The verdant forest, mighty mountains and old colonial buildings add to the charm of visiting this place..
Hiking and trekking to nearby places, shopping, discovering beautiful buildings of colonial era and churches are some places to visit here




Mussoorie, Uttarakhand


Picked by many as their first choice for a romance in the mountains, Mussoorie is certainly one of the finest Places for Honeymoon in India, It is also famous as an exceedingly Romantic Place among India’s most sought hill stations.
Breath-taking attractions of Mussoorie including Gun Hill, Kempty Falls, Camel’s Back Road, Jwalaji Temple etc.,




Kumarakom, Kerala


POne of the few places in the state to truly experience the beauty and wonders of Kerala’s lagoons and lovely backwaters, Kumarakom makes for an ideal honeymoon spot in India for the couples who seek peaceful togetherness.
Visit Vembanad Lake and Bird Sanctuary, stay in a romantic houseboat, explore the enchanting backwaters of Kerala




Dalhousie, Himachal Pradesh


Shillong is preferred mostly by the couples who wish to have a lot of privacy. The silent mountains and the calm clouds in Shillong give you more peace that you would expect, making it one of the most Beautiful places in India for Honeymoon.
Things to Do: Mawsynram, Mawlynnong, Umiam Lake, Elephant Falls, Shillong Peak, Ward’s Lake, David Scott Trail etc.





Conclusion:


The beauty of spending Honeymoon in India is you get to experience a diverse lifestyle. India has some of the best Honeymoon destinations in India on a budget. With amazing options, you can surely find a romantic spot to spend some quality time. So, pack your bags and book tickets to these places to have the best experiences of your life
